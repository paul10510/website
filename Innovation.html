Sand Pool Project


The sand pool project was my first project to make headlines. I had
seen a great video from the Youtuber Mark Rober,
and wanted to recreate what he did, but on a smaller scale. I did this
project in my middle school's Innovation Center, and the video my teacher
took of the project went on the school website.
