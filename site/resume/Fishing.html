Fishing

Personal Fishing

As most fisherman do, I do most of my fishing on my own time, when I want to. I usually
fish for Bass, but sometimes I will fish for Carp or Sunfish. I have only been deep
sea fishing three times. One of these trips was a sailing trip for 6 days at
SeaBase in the Florida keys with my Scout troop. We sailed out in the Florida keys
for 6 days only stoping at a marina once. We got to catch many fish including a bluefin
tuna I caught!

Professional Fishing

I also do a lot of fishing as part of my summer job at a local day camp.
I work there as a fishing specialist teaching kids from the ages of 5 to 13 the basics of fishing.
There we normally catch Sunfish, but the more experienced fishermen will also catch
some largemouth Bass. There are also times when the kids will catch Snapping Turtles,
and I must deal with those (we usually find some other lake where we can release them and they
will not hurt anyone).
