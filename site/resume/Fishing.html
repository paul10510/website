Fishing

Personal Fishing

As most fisherman do, I do most of my fishing on my own time, when I want to. I usually
fish for Bass, but sometimes I will fish for Carp or Sunfish. I have only been deep
sea fishing once. I went with my friend, his older brother, and my dad in Rhode Island.
That day I caught two Sea Robins, and some Black Sea Bass.

Professional Fishing

I also do a lot of fishing as part of my summer job at a local day camp.
I work there as a fishing counselor teaching kids from the ages of 5 to 13 the basics of fishing.
There we normally catch Sunfish, but the more experienced fishermen will also catch
some Smallmouth Bass. There are also times when the kids will catch Snapping Turtles,
and I must deal with those.
