Robotics

I was an inaugural member of the Rippowam Cisqua Robotics Team (Ripp Robotics).
The team began while I was in 6th grade and I participated through 8th grade.
It was part of the First Lego League (FLL) program.

My job on the team was to program the robots. In my 3 years on the team, I
programmed a sumo-wrestling bot and coded many of the jobs that the robot
did during our competitions.

Each year we participated in Robotics competitions with teams from all over Westchester
County, Putnam County and Dutchess County, NY. We came in 4th place twice!
