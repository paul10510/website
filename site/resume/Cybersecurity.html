Cybersecurity Club

I joined the Fordham prep Cybersecurity Club during my freshmman year. I fell in love with cybersecurity and want to
pursue it as a career. As a Sophomore I was the Co-President of the Cybersecurity Club, and now as a Junior/Senior I am the
President of the Cybersecurity Club.

Competitions

Under my leadership, the Cybersecurity Club competed for the first time in six years. We competed in the National
Cyber League Competition. There are 9 competitiors that make up two teams.
This year the club is planning to participate in some HackTheBox competitions.
My hope is to inspire these members with a love of Cybersecurity that rivals my own.

Meetings

Our Cybersecurity Club meetings cover many different topics and can range from a discussion,
to me bringing in one of my hacking tools and doing a demonstration, or even a presentation from a
professional in the field. This year we are have had two speakers so far, but plan to have more in the future!

Outside Activities

In order to educate myself about the Cybersecurity world and be a productive president for my club
I have participated in many outside activities to gain the knowledge that I present to my club members.
I participated in The National Student Leadership Conference in Washington DC with a focus on Cybersecurity.
I also attended a class on AI with Fordham University called "Future Leaders in AI", and lastly I took a class
with Columbia University's highschool program on Blockchain technology.
